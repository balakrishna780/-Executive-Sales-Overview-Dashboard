# 🛒 Executive Sales Overview Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Executive Sales Overview Dashboard** is a Power BI dashboard designed to provide business executives with a comprehensive overview of retail sales performance. It transforms raw sales data into actionable insights through interactive KPI cards, charts, and slicers, enabling data-driven business decisions.

This dashboard focuses on monitoring overall sales performance, profitability, customer metrics, and operational efficiency in a single, easy-to-understand interface.

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 📦 Category-wise Sales
Compares sales performance across different product categories to identify the highest revenue-generating categories.

---

### 🚚 Orders by Priority
Displays the distribution of orders based on priority levels, helping businesses understand operational workload.

---

### 👥 Sales by Segment
Analyzes sales contribution from different customer segments, enabling better customer targeting strategies.

---

### 📈 Yearly Sales Trend
Shows sales performance over time, helping identify business growth trends and seasonal patterns.

---

### 🏆 Top 10 Products by Sales
Highlights the highest-selling products based on total revenue generated.

- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales.
- Medium priority orders account for the majority of all orders.
